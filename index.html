Mizan

Know the fair price before you pay — what UAE residents actually pay, laid over advertised list prices.

Visit live site — mizan-price.com
Role
Solo — design & build
Type
Consumer, Web
Coverage
All 7 emirates
Status
Live, UAE

The problem

Across the UAE, the price you're quoted for everyday services — AC cleaning, a haircut, movers, a gym — often isn't what people actually pay. Markups and opaque pricing leave residents with no reference point, and no way to tell whether a quote is fair or steep.

How it works

Mizan — ميزان, "the scale" — shows two layers side by side: the market estimate (advertised list prices harvested from the web) and the paid range (what residents report actually paying). Pick a service and your area, enter the price you were quoted, and it returns a verdict: fair, steep, or walk away.

The paid layer is community-reported and anonymous, with outlier trimming so one odd price can't skew the result — and it sharpens with every report. Free to use, no paid placements, across all seven emirates.

What I did

Defined the concept and the two-layer data model, designed the full interface, and built and shipped it as a live product — including an Arabic version. The whole loop, from idea to something residents check before they pay.

Orfyx

A premium fitness-pass platform — find a verified gym, get a QR pass, and walk straight in. No contracts.

Visit live site — orfyx.com
Role
Solo — design & build
Type
Consumer, Web & app
Markets
UAE & India
Status
Live

The problem

Getting into a gym is heavier than it should be — tours, sales pitches, contracts, lock-ins. For anyone who moves between cities, or just wants to train without committing for a year, the membership model itself is the friction.

The approach

Orfyx turns it into a pass you request in seconds. Every gym is ID-verified before it goes live; you pick an activity and area, choose a pass — day, week or monthly — and walk in with a single QR scan. No paperwork, no lock-in, switch gyms mid-month.

On the other side, owners list a gym in about twenty minutes and verify entries with a one-handed scanner at reception — discovery and access without replacing their POS.

Outcome

Designed and built end to end — the consumer flow, the owner tools and the digital pass — with native iOS and Android, offline-cached passes and wallet support. Built across Dubai and Bengaluru, live in the UAE and India.
